Homepage of Axel Kölschbach Ortego

I'm a second year PhD student in Arithmetic Geometry at the Max-Planck Institute for Mathematics in Bonn. My advisor is David Hansen. You can send me an email to koelschbach at mpim-bonn dot mpg dot de.

Here is my CV. About my mathematical work:

  • Currently I am working on the completed cohomology of certain Harris–Taylor Shimura varieties.
  • In my Master Thesis, I constructed the determinant line bundle on the Ainf affine Grassmanian and on Scholze's Bdr affine Grassmanian.
  • In my Bachelor thesis, I wrote an exposition on the transcedental motive of a surface, first introduced by Kahn, Murre, Pedrini. Then I applied it to Huybrechts' notion of constant cycle curves.
